Ceremony

The ceremony will be held at 3:30pm, May 28th in the Field of Dreams at Chico Hot Springs.

Dress casual, go all out, or somewhere in between. Weather can be variable in May so come prepared for sun, snow, or just straight up cold.

Afterwards the reception will be held in the Convention Center adjacent to the field. Dinner and dessert will be served. We are bypassing the dancing and plan on going straight to the hot springs for a soak. So bring a suit (preferably the swimming kind) and continue the celebration with us!
